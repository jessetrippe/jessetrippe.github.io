Northwind

Identity for a Burlington, WI garden center and farm

Description

Complete redesign from concept to brand for Wisconsin based Northwind Perennial Farm Nursery & Garden. The project required extensive market research and historical analysis of the company. The final product communicated the research journey to creating a stunning new design while keeping Northwind's traditions as inspiration for guiding principles. The design included letterhead, logo, and brand book.
