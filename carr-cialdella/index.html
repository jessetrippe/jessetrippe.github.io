Carr Cialdella

Portfolio website for a Chicago photography studio

Description

Carr Cialdella, an architectural and interiors photography studio, needed a home to showcase their beautiful photographs, advertise their small collection of books, and share their services with prospective clients. Along with another designer, I built a simple solution that accomplishes all three while also allowing easy updates by the Carr Cialdella team.
